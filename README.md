#  Flipkart Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Project Overview
This project is an interactive *Power BI Dashboard* developed to analyze Flipkart sales data. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, product categories, customer ratings, payment methods, and regional sales trends. It helps users understand business performance through data visualization.

---

##  Dashboard Features

- 📈 Total Sales Overview
- 💰 Average Sales
- ⭐ Average Customer Rating
- 📦 Total Quantity Sold
- 🌍 Sales by Region (Map Visualization)
- 📊 Top Product Categories
- 📅 Sales Trend Over Time
- 💳 Payment Method Analysis
- ⭐ Sales Performance by Customer Rating
- 📦 Quantity Sold by Product Category
- 🌎 Regional Sales Breakdown by Payment Method
- Interactive Filters (Payment Method)

---

##  Tools & Technologies

##  Dataset

The dataset contains information such as:

- Product Category
- Sales Amount
- Quantity Sold
- Customer Rating
- Payment Method
- Region
- Order Date

---

##  Key Insights

- Total Sales reached *10M*.
- Grocery category generated the highest sales.
- Debit Card and COD were the most preferred payment methods.
- Sales varied across different regions.
- Customer ratings helped analyze sales performance.
- Monthly sales trends highlighted business growth patterns.
